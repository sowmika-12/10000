Pawankalyan

Konidela Pawan Kalyan 2 September 1968 or 1971 is an Indian actor,politician,filmmaker, martial artist, and philanthropist who primarily works in Telugu cinema. Known for his unique acting style and mannerisms, he has a large fan base and a cult fol lowing.One of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2013 and is the recipient of a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award, and a Santosham Film Award.He is also the founder of the JSP.
